Dabhoi

This is a selection of images from the Mahudi Gate at Dabhoi in Gujarat. The gate was decorated c. 1220-1230 with depictions of various siddhas, gods and yogis, and includes what we believe to be the oldest known depictions of complex yoga postures. Dr Mallinson and Dr Bevilacqua took these pictures in March 2016 and will return to Dabhoi in 2017 in order to get better images.
